## Alexander Kozlov

**Data Scientist** at [Pepperstone](https://www.pepperstone.com/)

Welcome to my personal website and blog. Here I share my passion for exploring and solving real-world problems with mathematics, data analysis and programming. Starting from a background in applied physics and driven by this passion, I worked on applications of machine learning to extreme weather forecasting for the aviation industry. My current interests are personalisation and natural language processing in finance. On the scientific front I have led and contributed to 26 projects in atomic and plasma physics, biochemistry, X-ray imaging, statistics and data analysis.

In my spare time I write for [Towards Data Science](https://medium.com/@alexx.kozloff), take up [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/competitions) data science challenges and explore new developments in natural language processing.
